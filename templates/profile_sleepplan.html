Varför...

  • Undvik blått ljus en timme före sömn: Hjälper dig att somna snabbare genom att reglera melatoninproduktionen.
  • Sov djupare: Minskar ögonbelastning och ger en mer återhämtande sömn.

Fördelarna med bra sömn

  • Increased energy levels: Wake up feeling refreshed and ready to tackle the day.
  • Improved mood: Enjoy a more positive outlook and reduced irritability.
  • Enhanced cognitive function: Sharper focus, better memory, and improved decision-making.
  • Strengthened immune system: Better equipped to fight off illnesses and infections.
  • Healthy weight management: Reduced cravings for unhealthy foods and better metabolism regulation.
  • Lower risk of chronic diseases: Decreased risk of conditions like heart disease, diabetes, and stroke.
  • Faster recovery: Improved muscle repair and overall physical restoration during sleep.

nackdelarna med dålig sömn

  • Fatigue: Constant tiredness and lack of energy throughout the day.

  • Weight gain: Increased cravings for sugary and high-calorie foods, leading to weight gain.
  • Increased risk of chronic diseases: Higher likelihood of developing conditions like obesity, diabetes, and hypertension.
  • Impaired physical performance: Reduced coordination, slower reaction times, and decreased athletic performance.
